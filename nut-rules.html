NUT RULES — WOS STATE 2128

We don’t do NAP — we do NUT: No Unnecessary Trouble.

Welcome to the NUT page, where alliances collaborate to maintain harmony and competitiveness in State 2128. If your alliance chooses not to participate, you may opt out. By default, all alliances are opted in.

NUT Rules

Freedom of Speech

  • No restrictions on speech in world or private chats.
  • If someone offends you, use the blacklist feature to block them.

City Attacks

  • Furnace level 20 and below can attack anyone anytime.
  • Do not repeatedly attack the same city to zero them.
  • Scouting: Allowed at all times.

Alliance Structures

  • Alliance headquarters cannot be attacked.
  • Alliance banners cannot be attacked unless they block access to facilities.
  • If banners block access: contact the R5/R4 before attacking and allow up to 24 h to move them.

Tile Attacks

  • Allowed only during Kill Event (KE).
  • If a player gathers in another alliance’s territory, those tiles can be attacked.

Scheduled Battles

  • Alliances can schedule fights by agreement.
  • No attacking outside of scheduled time.

Presidency Rotation

  • Rotates among the top 10 alliances in NUT.
  • “Whales” may switch alliances to help hold the castle.
  • Rallies on the Sunfire Castle are prohibited; solo attacks allowed.

Whale Distribution

  • “Whales” should spread out during major events like Castle Battle for balance.

Fortress/Stronghold Distribution

  • Rotates among alliances with at least 50 active members.
  • Alliances may trade fortresses/strongholds based on reward preferences.
  • Any alliance not assigned one may attack any available fortress/stronghold.

State vs State (SvS)

  • Players may switch alliances to help in SvS.
  • Players must return to their original alliance afterward (unless staying by choice & capacity).
  • R5s share the SvS plan — follow it; don’t embarrass yourself.

Recruitment

  • World chat and PM recruitment is always allowed.
  • If members leave, it’s a leadership issue, not a recruitment problem.
  • No retaliation against alliances that recruit from others.

Facilities

  • All facilities are free-for-all.
  • No blocking alliances from “touching” facilities.
  • No alliance should control more than one of the same facility type at once (e.g. two Lv1 Tech Facilities don’t stack — share it).

Brothers in Arms Rules

Brothers in Arms is a two-day PvP event where alliances battle by attacking enemy cities & troops. Earn points by killing or severely injuring troops; rewards depend on individual and alliance ranks. Coordinate offense & defense while protecting your own resources.

  • Shield or Fight: If you don’t want to participate, shield up. Stay in your alliance territory.
  • City Attacks: Max 2 attacks per city; if it’s burning, move on.
  • Alliance Structures: HQs and banners are off-limits.
  • Tile Combat: Resource tiles are fair game.
  • No Facility/Bear Interference: Don’t attack alliances during facility or bear fights.
  • Team Strategy: Group attacks OK within limits.
  • Respect Weaker Players: Attack only within ±3 Furnace levels.

Opt-Out Information

Alliances may opt out of NUT by notifying the leadership council. Opting out removes NUT protection — others may engage freely. Make sure it fits your alliance goals first.
